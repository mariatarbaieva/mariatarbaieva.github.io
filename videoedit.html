Video Share

Company: Dev-Pro
Role: UI/UX designer

Video Share is a tool that enables users to network, edit, and distribute video content.

Responsibilities

I collaborated with a project manager, a business analyst, a lead developer, an iOS developer, and a markup developer to discover insights and transform concepts into customer-centric features. I also designed wireframes and prototypes to communicate our vision and design principles, facilitating idea visualization, team discussions, and decision-making.

Wireframes sketching

I utilized sketches as a communication tool with the team. We needed to determine how to combine the original, auto-highlighted, and video duration editing options. The solution was to employ a segment control and separate these three options into three tabs.
