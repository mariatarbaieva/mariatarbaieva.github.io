Design System

Company: Cobalt
Role: Lead, Co-creator

The existing Cobalt design system remains integrated within the Cobalt app. This system includes functional code, design tools, valuable resources, human interface guidelines, and, being a federated design system, it also benefits from contributions by community members.

Problem

  • • The lack of visual guidelines caused delays and confusion

  • • Inconsistencies due to copying styles from different parts of the application

  • • Redesign of the app expected by leadership would require application rewrite first

Result

Implementing a design system can be challenging, especially when dealing with legacy components. Over the course of about 2 years, we successfully integrated the design system across the entire application. Our progress was diligently monitored through Jira, and we conducted regular sync-ups within the design system guild to ensure alignment among contributors.

I took the lead in establishing the initial workflows for the design system and advocated for its implementation within the organization. I also ensured that dedicated time was allocated for design and engineering teams to focus on the design system. Eventually, I handed over the responsibility of this initiative to a colleague and took on a contributor role. The development and enhancement of a design system are ongoing endeavors, and we continue to refine it, aiming for further advancement. You can explore the current state of the design system here.
